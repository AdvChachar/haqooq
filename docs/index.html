What is Haqooq?

Haqooq is a free, AI-powered legal aid app built for Pakistani citizens. It helps you understand Pakistani laws — FIR filing, bail procedures, family law, property rights, labor law, and more — in simple language. Available in Urdu and English.

Features

AI Legal Assistant

Ask legal questions and get simple, accurate answers about Pakistani law.

Urdu & English

Full bilingual support — switch between Urdu and English anytime.

FIR & Bail Guidance

Step-by-step help with FIR filing, bail applications, and court procedures.

Family & Property Law

Understand marriage, divorce, inheritance, and property rights under Pakistani law.

Labor Rights

Know your workplace rights — salary, overtime, termination, and more.

100% Free

No hidden charges. Legal help should be accessible to everyone.
